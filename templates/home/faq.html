Booking & Tickets

Booking a bus ticket on TravelMate is simple and straightforward:

  1. Visit our website or download the mobile app
  2. Enter your departure and destination cities
  3. Select your travel date
  4. Choose from available buses and timings
  5. Select your preferred seats
  6. Enter passenger details
  7. Make payment using your preferred method
  8. Receive your e-ticket via email and SMS

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking subject to the cancellation policy:

  • Cancellations made 24+ hours before departure: 10% cancellation fee
  • Cancellations made 12-24 hours before: 25% cancellation fee
  • Cancellations made 6-12 hours before: 50% cancellation fee
  • Cancellations made less than 6 hours before: No refund

To cancel or modify, visit 'My Bookings' in your account or contact customer support.

After successful booking and payment, you will receive your e-ticket through:

  • Email to your registered email address
  • SMS to your registered mobile number
  • Download from your account dashboard
  • TravelMate mobile app

Please ensure you carry a printed copy or show the digital ticket on your mobile device while boarding.

Payments & Refunds

We currently accept payments via:

  • Digital Wallet (eSewa)

All payments are processed securely with bank-level encryption.

Refund processing time:

  • Digital Wallet (eSewa): 1-2 business days

You will receive an email confirmation once the refund is processed. If you don't receive your refund within the specified time, please contact our customer support.

Bus Services

We offer various types of buses to suit different preferences and budgets:

  • AC Buses: Air-conditioned comfort for hot weather travel
  • Non-AC Buses: Budget-friendly option with natural ventilation
  • Sleeper Buses: Comfortable beds for overnight journeys
  • Semi-Sleeper: Reclining seats for comfortable day travel
  • Luxury Buses: Premium amenities and extra comfort

Each bus type comes with different amenities like WiFi, charging points, entertainment systems, and refreshments.

Account & Profile

Creating an account is quick and easy:

  1. Click on 'Sign Up' on our website or app
  2. Enter your email address and create a password
  3. Provide your basic information (name, phone number)
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Complete your profile setup

Having an account allows you to track bookings, save payment methods, and receive personalized offers.

Mobile App

The TravelMate mobile app is available for both Android and iOS devices:

  • Android: Download from Google Play Store
  • iOS: Download from Apple App Store

You can also scan the QR code on our website to directly download the app on your device. The app offers all website features plus exclusive mobile-only deals and notifications.
