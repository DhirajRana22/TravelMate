Our Story

TravelMate is a college project developed in 2024 as part of our academic curriculum to demonstrate modern web development skills and create a practical solution for bus travel booking in Nepal. This project showcases our understanding of full-stack development and user experience design.

As students, we identified the challenges of booking bus tickets in Nepal - long queues, lack of information about bus amenities, and uncertainty about schedules. We designed this platform as our final project to demonstrate how technology can solve real-world problems and provide travelers with a better booking experience.

Today, TravelMate connects thousands of travelers with hundreds of bus operators across Nepal, offering a wide range of options to suit every budget and preference. From the bustling streets of Kathmandu to the serene hills of Pokhara, we continue to innovate and improve our services.

Mission & Vision

Driving the future of bus travel in Nepal with innovation and dedication

Our Mission

To revolutionize the bus travel industry in Nepal by providing a transparent, reliable, and user-friendly platform that connects travelers with quality bus services. We aim to make bus travel the preferred mode of transportation across Nepal by enhancing the booking experience and ensuring customer satisfaction at every step of the journey.

Our Vision

To become the leading bus booking platform in Nepal and the region, known for innovation, reliability, and exceptional customer service. We envision a future where bus travel across Nepal is seamless, comfortable, and accessible to all, contributing to sustainable transportation solutions and connecting communities throughout the country.
