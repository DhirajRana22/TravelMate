You can track your booking status by logging into your TravelMate account and navigating to the "My Bookings" section. Alternatively, you can use the booking reference number sent to your email to check the status on our website or mobile app, or call our customer service at +977 9847435111.

To request a refund, log into your TravelMate account, go to "My Bookings," select the booking you wish to cancel, and click on the "Request Refund" button. Follow the instructions to complete the refund request. Please note that refund policies vary depending on the bus operator and how close to the departure date the cancellation is made. For specific refund information, please contact our support team at +977 9847435111 or email info@travelmate.com.
