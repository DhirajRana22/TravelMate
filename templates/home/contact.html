Frequently Asked Questions

You can track your booking status by logging into your TravelMate account and navigating to the "My Bookings" section. Alternatively, you can use the booking reference number sent to your email to check the status on our website or mobile app.

To request a refund, log into your TravelMate account, go to "My Bookings," select the booking you wish to cancel, and click on the "Request Refund" button. Follow the instructions to complete the refund request. Please note that refund policies vary depending on the bus operator and how close to the departure date the cancellation is made.

Yes, you can change your travel date for most bookings. Log into your account, go to "My Bookings," select the booking you wish to modify, and click on the "Change Date" option. Please note that date changes are subject to availability and may incur additional charges depending on the bus operator's policy.
