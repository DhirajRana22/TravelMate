When you make a booking through TravelMate, you are entering into a contract with the Bus Operator, not with TravelMate. We act as an intermediary, facilitating the booking process and providing customer support.

By making a booking, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate passenger information

  • Comply with the Bus Operator's terms and conditions

6. Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your booking through your TravelMate account or by contacting our customer support. The refund amount for cancelled bookings depends on how close to the departure time you are cancelling and the Bus Operator's cancellation policy.

General cancellation policy:

  • Cancellations made more than 48 hours before departure: 90% refund
  • Cancellations made 24-48 hours before departure: 75% refund
  • Cancellations made 12-24 hours before departure: 50% refund
  • Cancellations made less than 12 hours before departure: 25% refund or no refund (depending on the Bus Operator)

Please note that the TravelMate service fee is non-refundable in most cases. The exact refund amount will be displayed at the time of cancellation.

In case of cancellations by the Bus Operator, we will assist you in getting a full refund or making alternative arrangements.
